## 💬 Answers to Expected Questions

### Q: "How does the AI generate insights?"
**A:** *"It uses Google Gemini AI. I extract statistical patterns and model results, then send a structured prompt to Gemini which generates a professional narrative report in business language."*

### Q: "What makes this different from other ML tools?"
**A:** *"Three things: 1) AI-powered insight discovery that explains WHY, not just WHAT, 2) Automatic data quality assessment, and 3) End-to-end automation from upload to professional reports."*

### Q: "Can it handle different types of data?"
**A:** *"Yes, it supports CSV, Excel, and JSON files. It automatically detects data types and adapts the analysis. The sample datasets demonstrate this with different domains."*

### Q: "How accurate are the models?"
**A:** *"It compares 5 algorithms and selects the best one. On the Iris dataset, it achieved 96.7% accuracy. The system provides detailed metrics including precision, recall, and F1-score."*

### Q: "Is this production-ready?"
**A:** *"The backend uses FastAPI which is production-ready. I can easily add authentication, database connectivity, and cloud deployment. The AI insights make it valuable for both data scientists and business users."*
